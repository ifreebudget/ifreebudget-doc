Personal Finance Manager

Dec 9th, 2013

iFreeBudget is a Free Personal Finance Manager for Windows, Linux, Mac and Android platforms.

  • Track expenses and manage your budgets with its intuitive user interface and ability to import bank and credit card statements from Google Drive.
  • Create monthly and weekly budgets and track expenses based on budget to see where you are spending most money.
  • Fully customizable accounts and categories. Supports creating nested categories and accounts easily.
  • Weekly or monthly cash flow report for overall picture of your finances (press menu on main screen).
  • Recurring expenses or incomes with reminders.
  • Save backups to your Google Drive.
  • Share backups between multiple devices to keep them in sync.
  • Export reports to Google Docs as spreadsheets.
